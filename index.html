Enroot

What is Enroot?
Enroot is a location based social networking application.

Concept
Life is a beautiful journey. In this journey, we create and share countless memories at different junctions. Enroot provides you the best way to experience your past memories, every time you revisit that junction.

Sustainability

To study the problems of the Village "Barduwa" and provide potential and sustainable solutions

About Burduwa?
It is a small village in the state of Assam in India. This village famous for 'Than', which means a sacred place in Assamese society. It is the birthplace of Srimanta Sankaradeva; the great artist, literateur, dramatist and the founder of Vaisnavism in Assam.

Focus Areas
Culture (Vaishnavism)
Transportation
Health and Sanitation
Agriculture
